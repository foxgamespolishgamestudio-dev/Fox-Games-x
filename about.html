About Fox Games

Fox Games is an independent studio founded with a passion for horror games that immerse players in tense, atmospheric worlds. Our goal is to combine storytelling, exploration, and psychological tension to create unforgettable experiences.

Our Vision

We believe that games are a medium for emotion. Horror games, in particular, can evoke fear, curiosity, and excitement. At Fox Games, we aim to craft experiences that challenge the mind and the senses, building suspense, mystery, and tension that remain with players long after they finish the game.

Despite being a small team, we focus on attention to detail, from lighting and sound design to environmental storytelling, ensuring our games feel alive and immersive.

Our Commitment

Fox Games is committed to creating high-quality, story-driven horror games. We take pride in building immersive worlds where players feel tension, curiosity, and excitement. Each project is a step toward crafting experiences that resonate with players around the world.
