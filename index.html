FOX GAMES - POLISH GAMES STUDIO

We are an independent horror game studio passionate about immersive storytelling, tense atmospheres, and unforgettable experiences. Every project we create is designed to keep players on the edge of their seat.

Our Mission

At Fox Games, we aim to redefine horror experiences for players of all ages. We combine exploration, psychological tension, and rich narrative elements to craft worlds that feel alive, mysterious, and terrifyingly immersive.

Even as a small studio, we focus on detail — every room, shadow, and sound is carefully designed to provoke curiosity and fear. Our mission is to leave a lasting impression on players, making every game memorable and unique.

Featured Game: HOME SWEET HOME 1

Our first game is a psychological horror set in a dark and abandoned orphanage. Floors from -10 to -30 are sealed, hiding secrets and children who are no longer human. Players explore, survive, and unravel the mystery that haunts the orphanage.

Status: In Development

Why We Create Horror

Horror is more than jump scares — it’s about atmosphere, storytelling, and psychological tension. Our games challenge the mind, evoke curiosity, and provoke emotions that linger long after the gameplay ends.

We aim to create immersive worlds where every sound, shadow, and encounter contributes to a larger narrative. Players are not just observers; they are part of the environment, and their choices influence how the story unfolds.
