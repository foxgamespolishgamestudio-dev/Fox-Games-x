FOX GAMES

We are an independent horror game studio passionate about immersive storytelling, tense atmospheres, and unforgettable experiences. Every project we create is designed to keep players on the edge of their seat.

Our Mission

At Fox Games, we aim to redefine horror experiences for players of all ages. We combine exploration, psychological tension, and rich narrative elements to craft worlds that feel alive, mysterious, and terrifyingly immersive.

Even as a small studio, we focus on detail — every room, shadow, and sound is carefully designed to provoke curiosity and fear. Our mission is to leave a lasting impression on players, making every game memorable and unique.
